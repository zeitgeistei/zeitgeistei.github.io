The Far Lands

The Farlands of Computing. A bunch of free information able to be accessed by anyone. Rich or poor. Your social status, identity, anyone can access this page.

Da Rulez

  • Do not use this information to mess with property that isn't yours (i.e. school issued devices like Chromebooks)
  • Encyclopedic information here may be outdated.
  • Contribution rules are different.

Contribution Rulez

  • Cite primary and secondary sections at the bottom of your page.
  • Do not use fancy CSS
  • Do not 'go after' someone who has contributed to this page. This reinforces safety for me and the sites contributors.

What Gets Accepted

  • People that respond quickly.
  • Articles that contain references and sources.
  • Bugfixes that were tested on multiple platforms, different browser, different device, Incognito mode

What Gets Rejected

  • Information that will clearly be used to commit cybercrime
  • Content subject to copyright
  • Ideas that can be merged with another article.
